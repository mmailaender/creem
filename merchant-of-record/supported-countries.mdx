Creem supports purchases from all countries except those in the [unsupported list](#unsupported-countries-for-purchases) below. We provide payouts to merchants in **86 countries**.

<Info>
  If you don't see your country listed below,
  sorry, you won't be able to use Creem at this time. We are always expanding our
  region support, and would love to hear from you.
</Info>

### Payout Methods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Local Bank Transfer" icon="building-columns">
    Available in **all 86 supported countries**. Payout fee: 7 EUR/USD or 1% of the payout amount, whichever is higher.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stripe Connect" icon="stripe-s">
    Available in **35 countries** (mostly EU/EEA). Payouts via your connected Stripe account. No Payout fee.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
